Bio

Initially, when I was an elementary teacher in 2013-2014, I would hang out with a coworker and we would play with coding sites like Codecademy. It felt challenging and rewarding to build things on the web. To basically be able to build whatever we want. In 2016, I went all in. I used a myriad of free and low cost coding sites and finally I finished Thinkful's coding bootcamp. As a graduated philosophy major, I appreciate the logic and ruled basis of it all yet all the creativity that is possible in the universe of web development.
